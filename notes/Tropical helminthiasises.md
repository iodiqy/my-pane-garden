# Overview

## Helminth infections
- Nematodes (roundworms)
	- Ascariasis
	- Enterobiasis etc.
- Cestodes (tapeworms)
	- Taeniasis
	- Diphyllobothriasis (fish tapeworm infection)
- Trematodes (flukes)
	- *Schistosomiasis*
	- Clonorchiasis
	- Paragonimiasias
	- Fascioliasis


## Schistosomiasis
- **Pathogen**
	- Schistosoma mansoni
	- Schistosoma haematobium
	- Schistosoma japonicum
- **Mode of transmission**
	- Penetration of the skin by a larvae (личинки)
	- Freshwater snails are intermediate hosts
- **Clinical features**
	- Pruritic maculopapular rash (swimmer's itch)
	- Genitourinary (GU) symptoms
		- Hematuria
		- Dysuria
		- Can cause bladder carcinoma
	- GI symptoms
		- Diarrhea
		- Abdominal pain
		- Hepatosplenomegaly
		- Periportal fibrosis
		- Portal hypertension
	- Pulmonary symptoms
		- Pulmonary hypertension
		- Cor pulmonale
- **Diagnosis**
	- Eggs in stool or urine
	- Serology
	- Eosinophilia
- **Treatment**
	- Praziquantel (Antihelmintic)
	- Corticosteroids
