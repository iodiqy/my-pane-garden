# Japanese encephalitis lecture notes

- Region: Asia
- Vector: Mosquite
- Mechanism of transmission: Transmissive
- Transmission: Hemocontact
- Clinical features (almost same as in [[Tick-Borne encephalitis]])
	- Rash
	- More aggressive
- Treatment
	- Immunoglobulins
